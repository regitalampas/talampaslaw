Careers at TLFTA

For aspiring lawyers looking to grow through real legal work

We support law students, legal interns, and early-career lawyers who want to learn from active litigation and advisory teams while building practical legal judgment with mentorship and discipline.

View Open Roles How to Apply Mentorship-first team

Who we are looking for

You should have strong ethics, a service-first mindset, and a willingness to do detailed legal work. We value curiosity, clear communication, and steady preparation.

Law students & graduates

Candidates preparing for bar admission or early into law school projects can find supervised opportunities to strengthen legal research, drafting, and case analysis skills in a structured setting.

Aspiring associates

New associates can develop stronger client communication and courtroom preparation habits by working with active cases and receiving direct review from senior lawyers.

Legal support talent

Applicants with legal operations, case management, or documentation strengths can grow through hands-on assignments in high-impact legal workflows.

Our hiring approach for law graduates

We prioritize readiness to learn over polished perfection.

  1. 1

    Submit your profile

    Send your CV, a short motivation note, and supporting details about coursework or internships.

  2. 2

    Short review

    We assess legal reasoning, communication, and suitability for assigned tracks.

  3. 3

    Interview and case simulation

    Selected candidates discuss motivation and complete a short practical exercise.

  4. 4

    Onboarding

    You are assigned a supervisor and receive a mentorship-guided workflow for your first 90 days.

What we value in aspiring talent

  • Respectful and ethical client treatment
  • Meticulous drafting and file organization
  • Initiative in research and follow-through
  • Commitment to continuous professional growth
