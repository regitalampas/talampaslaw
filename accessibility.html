Commitment to Accessibility

The Law Firm of Talampas & Associates is committed to making this website accessible and usable for as many people as possible.

Current Accessibility Practices

  • Semantic heading structure and clear content sections.
  • Keyboard-accessible links and controls.
  • Alternative text for meaningful images.
  • Responsive layout for desktop and mobile devices.
  • Readable text contrast for primary interface elements.

Known Limitations

Some legacy pages or media content may not yet fully meet every accessibility expectation. We continue improving accessibility as updates are made.
