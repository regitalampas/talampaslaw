Our Firm

About Us

The Law Firm of Talampas & Associates started as a small litigation practice built on trial discipline, client trust, and principled legal service. Over the years, we expanded into a multi-practice team while preserving the same founding commitment: practical legal strategy delivered with integrity.

How the Firm Began

In its earliest years, the firm focused on resolving complex disputes for individuals and small businesses in Makati. This early courtroom work shaped the way we continue to practice today: prepare deeply, communicate clearly, and advocate decisively when clients are at critical turning points.

Firm Milestones

Founding Period

A focused litigation practice is established to support clients facing urgent civil and family disputes.

Practice Expansion

The firm broadens to labor, corporate, and cybercrime matters as client needs become more interdisciplinary.

Team Growth

Additional lawyers join to strengthen trial readiness, client advisory services, and legal research depth.

Modern Service Model

Operations evolve with digital workflows, faster client communication, and preventive legal planning support.

Enduring Principles

Our history is grounded in values that have remained constant through every stage of growth.

  • Faithful and ethical representation in every engagement.
  • Disciplined preparation before negotiation, filing, or trial.
  • Transparent communication with realistic legal options.
  • Long-term protection of each client's rights and interests.
